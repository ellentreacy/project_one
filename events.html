The green heart of North Dublin, St. Annes is central to keeping residents connected and active.

See below for a list of groups currently running or sign up to our Newsletter to keep up to date!

Email Jane at parks@dcc.ie with any questions or for more information.

Noticing Nature


The Noticing Nature group offers a safe and nurturing environment in which you can learn more about the natural world. Sessions are seasonally themed and include nature walks, animal surveys and plant identification workshops.
Sessions last for 2 hours and include a coffee break either in the middle or at the end so that participants can get to know eachother. Each session is designed to be self-contained so don't worry about coming every week - drop in whenever you feel!

Noticing Nature runs on a Monday morning 10am-12pm

Community Gardens


Help us to manage the St. Annes allotment. The site contains raised beds, fruit trees, a polytunnel and a wildflower meadow. The Community Gardens group is peer-led and all levels of green-fingers are encouraged. Seed and skill sharing is welcomed!

The group meets every Wednesday and Sunday morning - officially from 10:30am-12pm, but gardeners are welcome to stay on if they wish.

EcoWarriers


EcoWarriers is a youth group to connect those interested in all things environmental. EcoWarriers currently has two groups - EcoConnect and EcoAwareness.

EcoConnect is a group for ages 7-11. The group seeks to engage youths with nature through learning skills such animal tracking, plant identification and foraging. Every third week Bushcraft is taught by Wolf.

EcoAwareness is a group for 12+. Running similar activities to EcoConnect, this group also teaches the importance of conservation and sustainability. Peer-learning is encouraged and keen environmentalists are always welcome to share what's important to them with the group

EcoConnect meet every Thursday at 5pm at the Red Stables.
EcoAwareness meet every Tuesday at 6pm at the Red Stables.

Coffee Catch-Up


Meet up with your fellow park goers for a coffee and a chat every Tuesday and Saturday. Bring your own or venture to the Red Stables for a cuppa. Everyone and anyone welcomed with a smile :)

Meet at the carved tree by the Red Stables at 11am
